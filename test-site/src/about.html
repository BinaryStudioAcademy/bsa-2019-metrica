Photo World, founded in 1947, is internationally renowned as one of the world's leading independent picture agencies. We take pride in our long history and our ongoing association with some of the greatest names in photography; names that include Yousuf Karsh, Sorci and Cecil Beaton.
We are also a home for top contemporary photographers such as Andy Gotts, Jason Bell, John Swannell, Perou and Jillian Edelstein. We produce news, quality celebrity studio shoots, and top travel and reportage features on a daily basis. As well as our rich archive of royal and historical pictures, our remit also covers entertainment, stock and portraiture, and a specialist lifestyle department, focusing on interiors, beauty, food, fitness and fashion.
